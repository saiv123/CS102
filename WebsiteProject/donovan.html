Bloedel Donovan Park

About

Bloedel Donovan Park is on the shore of Lake Whatcom. During the summer lots of people are here swimming and jumping off the nearby bridge into the water. Picnic tables and grills are available to reserve. There is also a basketball court and volleyball court, as well as a boat launch.

Hours

6:00am to 10:00pm
