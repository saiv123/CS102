Lake Padden Park

About

Lake Padden is a large park with many activities available. There are many back trails for off-leash dog walking and horses. the lake itself is stocked for fishing and is a popular lake for kayaking.

Hours

6:00am to 10:00pm

address

4882 S Samish Way, Bellingham, WA 98229

Directions

From campus catch the 196 WWU/Lincoln to the lincoln creek P&R. Then take the 533 Yew St to the Samish Way at E Lake Padden entrance.
