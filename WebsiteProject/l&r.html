Laws & Rights at public parks

Parks and recreation facilities are available for public use whenever possible, as long as there aren't any city programs happening or reservations. Private or special events require reservations. Permits are required for campfires, overnight camping, wood cutting, and any removal of natural resources on park property.

City parks are open to the public from 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Parks are closed from 10 p.m. to 6:00 a.m. Nobody can enter a closed park without permission, any violations are civil infractions.

more information on laws & rights at public parks may be found at the official city of Bellingham Website.

Park/Facility Rental Policies

All of the Bellingham parks mentioned above have the following rules and requirements:

  • Must be 18 to rent facilities or equipment from parks, responsible for all payment of fees.
  • Pay in full time of Reservation
  • Reservation can be made up to one year in advance
  • City Activities recieve first priority for facility use.
