Fairhaven Park

About

Fairhaven park has 136 acres to explore. It contains a large grass field, a playground, a labrynth, and trails.

Hours

6:00am to 10:00pm

Address

107 Chukanut Drive North Bellingham, WA 98225

Directions

Go to the bus stop just north of campus at Billy Frank Jr at Laurel St, get off the bus at old fairhaven parkway, then walk south on 14th st until you reach the park.
